# Thumbnail — THE STORY OF EVERYTHING

Documentary-poster style, NOT the white-card cartoon formula.

- Full-bleed cinematic illustration from the episode's strongest visual (the scene
  a viewer would pause on): dramatic scale contrast — a small human figure against
  a vast phenomenon (a lone astronomer under the galactic arc; a rowboat beside a
  breaching megafauna; a candlelit figure weighing the world).
- Palette: deep space blues/blacks + one warm gold accent. High contrast; must read
  at 168px wide.
- Composited text (deterministic layer, not generated): 2–4 word episode hook in the
  serif wordmark style, cream (#f2ead9), lower third or upper left — whichever the
  composition leaves empty. NEVER cover the focal subject.
- Logo bottom-left, small.
- One thumbnail concept per episode, unique composition every time — no template
  scene with swapped props.
